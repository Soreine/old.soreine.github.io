About

I am Soreine and I am passionate about everything related to video games. My real name is Nicolas Gaborit and I am currently finishing my university studies in computer science. I love programming, playing music, pixel art and generative art.

I consider video games as a genuine form of art. Sure they are generally crafted to be fun, but they often bring along emotions and memories in a unique way.
